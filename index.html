— THE WORLD —

Your quiet companion
at the edge of the world.

The world flooded a long time ago. What's left of it rises above the water, building by building. You're one of the ones still stacking floors, still trying to get a little higher. You fish to get materials. You build to see further.

It's a quiet game. Leave it running, check back when you want. Nothing punishes you for stepping away. The higher you get, the more stars show up that weren't there before.

— HOW IT WORKS —

A simple life · A growing world.

🎣

Fish for Everything

Cast your line at any depth. Set your target, wait, and pull up wood, fish, and rare treasures from the sea below.

🔨

Build Your Home

Trade your catch for materials and raise your house floor by floor. Every level changes the view around you.

📖

Collect & Remember

Fill your album with the fish you catch, the stars you find, and the memories you make along the way.

✦

Uncover the Mystery

Strange visitors arrive with questions. What lies beneath the sea? The higher you climb, the more the story reveals.
